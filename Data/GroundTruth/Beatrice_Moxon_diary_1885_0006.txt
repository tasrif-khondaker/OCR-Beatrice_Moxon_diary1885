At 11-45. Mddle. M. Hermine + Jeanne + Irma [UNCLEAR]
waiting for us. Discussed + eat tartines + figs
for 20 minutes. Then retired, very tired +
glad to tumble head first into bed. _
<u>Sunday</u> 18. Fine day. Got up rather late.
Went to church as usual, after went for a walk
by the park. Read in the afternoon. Girls pulled
me to pieces nearly. Ready + finished “Free
to Serve”  very interesting.
<u>Monday 19.</u> Fine day; got a sore throat coming,
Lessons as usual. Girls went to a lecture. 
After supper sat in the school-room, wretched 
+ slow, everything that’s nasty. Awfully homesick.
Elise brought me a letter + paper when I was in bed.
<u>Tuesday</u> 20 Very poorly. Throat feels vert bad
pain to swallow. Went to Gimy + put my costume
on. Very miserable until evening then all was put right as
<u>Wednesday</u> 21. Lessons as ever. My throat is worse.
Gertie awfully kind gave me Spanish etc etc. Did
not go out for a walk.
<u>Thursday 22</u>. Could not go out for my throat.
In the aftn. G. Dale + some of the others went out
with Mdlle Pauline. I couldnt go Gertie brought
me back a heap of things she had brought for me.
