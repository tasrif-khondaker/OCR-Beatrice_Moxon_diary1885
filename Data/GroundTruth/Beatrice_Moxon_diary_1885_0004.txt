In the evening amused ourselves + danced, returned late.
Sunday . Went to church in the morng. Church very
nicely decorated. After dinner we sat round the
fire + talked when we had a brilliant <u>“light”</u>
in our midst. I began to read Lady of Provence
by A. L. O. E.  The girls came back most of them.
I asked to sleep with in Mdlle Hermmie’s dortiec, 
“Yes! I may, oh! How jolly”; went to change my
Drawers, etc!
<u>Monday</u>. School begins once more, I must get settled
down to work. We did not go out for a walk.
Nothing striking happened.
<u>Tuesday</u>. Very wet day. Went to the Gimmy. Lessons
all day as usual. Had a dance after supper
awful stale, (reason, I won’t give!!) All the
girls turned daft wouldn’t dance.
<u>Wednesday</u>. Lessons as usual; stale chuck
Begin again + with it all unending monotony.
<u>Thursday</u>. Mdlle Pauline had a head-ache.
It is decided that old Letg[UNCLEAR] is to come + give us
lessons in Style. Went to have a dip.
felt very cold, after it !!!!!! (not had a bath for 3 no
<u>Friday</u> So dreadful awful fearful stale
Lessons as usual. Dreadful news from
home. Poor Uncle Parkin died last Tuesday. Mother + Pa gone to the funeral.
